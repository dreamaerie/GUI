So you think this is a joke? That this little goober ISN'T me? Well honestly I don't know what I was thinking when I drew this thing. I remember wanting a new profile picture for some SNS app, and the next thing I knew, this abomination was looking at me. Despite its horrifying nature, I am actually a big fan of it. It really does capture the struggle of life, the perputual act of pushing a large boulder up a hill day in and day out, only to have it roll back down right when you reach the top. And despite melting into a soupy puddle from the harsh rays of reality, this Goober has the power to give the world a thumbs up. Powerful stuff.


Anyways. Heres a real picture of me.

Things That I Would Give A Thumbs Up

Shrimp. I'm a big fan of shrimp. Shrimp is real good. So good in fact that I think the S in super stands for shrimp. Generally does not matter how its prepared most shrimp dishes are excellent. But if I had to pick one shrimp dish to survive off of for the rest of my days, it would be coconut shrimp. Just such a delightful dish. A perfect balance of savoury and sweet, and can be complimented by any sauce of ones choosing. Simply a perfect dish in my eyes. I only pity those with shellfish allergies.

I jive with good music. Typically I listen to J-rock and R&B, but I listen to a lot of different genres; It really just depends on my mood. Some days its a heavy metal day, other days its a classical music angle. My favorite musical artist would have to be Eve or Raito. Eve is a japanese singer/songwriter that makes a variety of different kinds of music, with a lot of bangers that I like. Raito on the other hand specializes in music for video games, which means that his music is usually high energy and very catchy. I like to listen to his music while working on projects and the sort, and depending on what songs I listen to the theme of my projects end up feeling like whatever I listened to at the time.
